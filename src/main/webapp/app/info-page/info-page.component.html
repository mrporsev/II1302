Plantalyzer

Plantalyzer is an IOT project that gives you updates about your houseplant' soil moisture,humidity and sunlight levels. This project has been achieved by group 15 in the course Projects and Project Methods at KTH university.

How To Use Plantalyzer?!

To use plantalyzer you will only need:
1-valid email address.
2-websurfer.
Step 1 Create a new account in Plantalyzer
Step 2 Log in with your account
Step 3 Go to the sensor page
Step 6 Now you are done with the process and you can finally see all the provided information that is observed by the sensors
Step 5 Add a new sensor to each plant that you want to monitor
Step 4 Press on add button to register your plant/s
